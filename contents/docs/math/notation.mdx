---
title: "Notation"
description: "Common notation used in mathematics and machine learning."
---

## Numeric Notation
- Scalar: $a$
- Vector: $\mathbf{a}$
- Matrix: $\mathbf{A}$
- Tensor: $\mathbf{A}$

## Set Notation
- Set: $A$
- Element: $a \in A$
- Subset: $A \subseteq B$
- Union: $A \cup B$
- Intersection: $A \cap B$
- Complement: $A^C$

## Function Notation
- Function: $f: A \to B$
- Domain: $\text{dom}(f)$
- Range: $\text{range}(f)$

## Calculus Notation
- Derivative: $\frac{d}{dx} f(x)$
- Integral: $\int f(x) dx$

## Operators
- Sum: $\sum$
- Product: $\prod$

## Probability Notation
- Probability: $P(A)$
- Conditional Probability: $P(A \mid B)$
- Joint Probability: $P(A, B)$
- Union: $P(A \cup B)$
- Intersection: $P(A \cap B)$
- Complement: $P(A^C)$
